# Job Application Packager Terms

Use these terms when packaging job applications for this user. They are meant to keep outputs concrete, natural, and evidence-backed.

## Required Terms

- `company-specific application package`: the full deliverable folder for one company and role.
- `tailored PDF resume`: the final role-specific resume artifact.
- `field-by-field answer document`: the Markdown document mapping visible form fields to suggested answers.
- `Ready to fill`: fields with answers supported by the resume, role page, or user-provided facts.
- `Needs user confirmation`: sensitive or uncertain fields that require the user's exact answer.
- `workflow discovery`: mapping a messy business process before automating it.
- `deployable AI workflow`: an automation or agent workflow that can be used by a real team.
- `guardrails`: explicit constraints that prevent unsafe, low-quality, or unsupported behavior.
- `operating pattern`: a reusable way of running work, not a one-off prompt.
- `enterprise-facing technical communication`: concise explanation for business and technical stakeholders.
- `measured outcome`: a quantified result tied to real prior work.
- `role-fit narrative`: the short through-line connecting the user's evidence to the target role.

## Do Not Use As Substitutes

- Do not say `AI transformation` unless it is tied to a named workflow, artifact, or measured outcome.
- Do not say `agentic engineering` without explaining the deployable workflow or operating pattern.
- Do not say `stakeholder management` when the stronger evidence is `enterprise-facing technical communication`.
- Do not invent SaaS ownership terms such as `Zendesk`, `Salesforce`, `ServiceNow`, `post-sales`, or `CSM` unless the source material supports them.
- Do not call a field `Ready to fill` if it asks for phone, work authorization, sponsorship, relocation, salary, demographic, compliance, legal, or other sensitive answers not already provided for that exact application.
- Do not describe generated files as submitted or uploaded.

## Preferred Output Phrases

- `Generated a company-specific application package with one tailored PDF resume and one field-by-field answer document.`
- `This answer is Ready to fill because it is supported by the role page and the source resume.`
- `This field Needs user confirmation because it asks for sensitive or exact personal information.`
- `The role-fit narrative emphasizes workflow discovery, deployable AI workflows, guardrails, and measured outcomes.`
